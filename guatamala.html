Guatamala
Guatemala, located in Central America, is a country with a rich history and diverse culture. Spanning from the Pacific Ocean to the Caribbean Sea, Guatemala is known for its breathtaking natural landscapes, ancient Mayan ruins, and vibrant indigenous communities. Environmental conservation is a growing concern in Guatemala, with efforts focused on protecting its unique biodiversity, including tropical rainforests and wildlife. However, deforestation, pollution, and the impact of climate change pose ongoing challenges to these preservation efforts.
