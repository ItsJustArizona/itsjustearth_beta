Northern Florida, USA
Northern Florida, United States of America
Northern Florida is a region that encompasses the northernmost part of the Sunshine State, bordering Georgia to the north and the Gulf of Mexico to the west. It is characterized by its diverse geography, rich history, and unique cultural blend. Economically, Northern Florida is driven by a mix of industries, including tourism, agriculture, and military bases. The region boasts vibrant cities like Jacksonville, which is the largest city by area in the contiguous United States, and Tallahassee, home to Florida State University and Florida A&M University.
